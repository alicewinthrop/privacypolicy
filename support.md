# Norwegian 4x4 Support

*Interval Training Timer for iPhone & Apple Watch*

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I set my max heart rate?**
Go to Settings and toggle "Manual Override" under Heart Rate Max. If you don't override it, the app estimates your HRmax from your age using the Tanaka formula (208 - 0.7 × age).

**Why isn't my heart rate showing during the workout?**
Heart rate data requires an Apple Watch paired and running the companion app. Make sure you've granted HealthKit permissions when prompted during onboarding.

**Can I change the interval durations?**
The 4x4 protocol uses fixed 4-minute intervals and 3-minute recoveries. You can adjust warm-up and cool-down durations in Settings.

**Where is my workout data stored?**
All workout data is saved to Apple Health on your device. The app has no server, no accounts, and no analytics. Your data never leaves your device.
