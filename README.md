  <h1>Norwegian 4x4 — Privacy Policy</h1>
  <p><strong>Effective date:</strong> <span class="placeholder">April 18, 2026</span></p>
  <p><strong>Developer:</strong> <span class="placeholder">Alice Winthrop</span></p>

  <h2>Overview</h2>
  <p>
    Norwegian 4x4 is a workout timer app for iPhone and Apple Watch. Your privacy
    is important — this app is designed so that your data stays on your device.
  </p>

  <h2>Data the App Accesses</h2>
  <p>With your explicit permission, Norwegian 4x4 reads and writes the following via Apple HealthKit:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Heart rate samples</li>
    <li>Workout sessions and summaries</li>
  </ul>
  <p>This data is stored in Apple Health on your device. The app does not maintain a separate database or copy of your health data.</p>

  <h2>Data Collection</h2>
  <p>Norwegian 4x4 does <strong>not</strong> collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Specifically:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>No analytics or crash-reporting SDKs are included.</li>
    <li>No advertising frameworks are included.</li>
    <li>No data is sent to any server, third party, or the developer.</li>
    <li>No user accounts are required or supported.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>HealthKit Data</h2>
  <p>
    Health data accessed through HealthKit is used solely to display workout
    metrics to you in real time and to save completed workouts to Apple Health.
  </p>
  <p>
    Your health data is <strong>never</strong> sold, shared with third parties,
    or used for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than providing app
    functionality directly to you.
  </p>

  <h2>Tracking</h2>
  <p>Norwegian 4x4 does not track you across apps or websites. The app does not use the App Tracking Transparency framework because there is nothing to track.</p>

  <h2>Third-Party Services</h2>
  <p>The app does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that process user data.</p>

  <h2>Children's Privacy</h2>
  <p>The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.</p>

  <h2>Changes to This Policy</h2>
  <p>If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.</p>
